I got this idea from <a href="http://joelrunyon.com/two3/the-impossible-list">Joel Runyon</a>, who believes in making an "Impossible List" instead of a "Bucket List". Bucket lists are static lists of things you know you want to get to in your life, where an Impossible List is an ever-evolving list of goals for your life; goals which you believe to be impossible or will require some effort.
